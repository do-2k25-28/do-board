## Responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:

- Review and merge pull requests
- Triage issues and feature requests
- Ensure CI stays green
- Keep dependencies up to date

## Becoming a maintainer

Consistent contributors who have had several pull requests merged may be invited to become maintainers. Reach out to an existing maintainer if you are interested.
